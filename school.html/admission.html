Admission Process

Welcome to the admission portal of Gurukul Aashram. We are excited to have you join our vibrant learning community. Please read the instructions below carefully before filling out the application form.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students must meet the age requirements for the respective class as per government guidelines.
  • Previous school's mark sheet and transfer certificate are required for admission from Class 2 onwards.
  • Admission is based on an entrance evaluation and an interview with the principal.

Documents Required

  • Passport size photographs of the student (4 copies).
  • Birth Certificate (Photocopy).
  • Aadhar Card of Student and Parents.
  • Previous year's report card.
  • Original Transfer Certificate (TC).
